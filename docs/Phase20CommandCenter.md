# Command Center

The command center aggregates CRM, delivery, revenue, and portal signals into one operating view.

## Inputs

- Lifecycle account metrics and due next actions.
- Research checklist gaps.
- Sales overdue tasks and weighted pipeline.
- Onboarding payment readiness.
- Execution delivery risks.
- Maintenance support and renewal risks.
- Revenue metrics.
- Portal unread messages.

## Removed Inputs

Outreach metrics, campaign counts, and send approval actions have been removed.
